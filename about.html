Our Legacy

Gulshan Public School, established in 2001, is one of the most trusted and respected educational institutions in Deoband, Uttar Pradesh. Our school is known for discipline, quality education, moral values and modern learning.

Our Mission

To shape students into confident, knowledgeable, responsible and innovative leaders of tomorrow.

To become the most cherished school by creating an environment where learning is joyful, discipline is natural and success is achieved with values.
